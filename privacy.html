Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb 15 2026

HatzChat does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Your API key is stored locally in your Mac Keychain. Your chat messages and files uploaded (if applicable) are stored locally and sent to Hatz AIfor processing.

The chat messages and files sent to Hatz AI are handled by them and subject to their privacy and retention policies.

No information you provide within the app (chats, keys, files) are sent to anyone other than Hatz AI, which is needed to use their services.

We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party SDKs of any kind.

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/jamesbabiak/HatzChat
